### 1.下载并解压LuaJIT

    
    
    http://luajit.org/download.html  
  
---  
  
### 2.修改为静态编译

    
    
    打开LuaJIT目录srcmsvcbuild.bat
    
    搜索替换LJARCH=x64为LJARCH=x86
    
    搜索替换/MD为/MT  
  
---  
  
### 3.使用VS命令提示符

    
    
    cd /d LuaJIT目录src
    
    编译DLL：msvcbuild.bat
    
    编译LIB：msvcbuild.bat static
