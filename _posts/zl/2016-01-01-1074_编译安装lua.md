下载:

    
    
    build $ wget https://www.lua.org/ftp/lua-5.1.5.tar.gz
    

解压:

    
    
    build $ tar xf lua-5.1.5.tar.gz
    

make:

    
    
    lua-5.1.5 $ make linux
    

安装:

    
    
    lua-5.1.5 $ sudo make install
